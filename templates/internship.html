💡 My Motivation

As an Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning student, I have always believed that learning extends beyond the classroom. While academic studies provided me with a strong theoretical foundation, I wanted to gain practical experience by working on real-world projects and understanding how technology solves real-life problems.

This motivation encouraged me to join the 6 Weeks AI & ML Internship at CodroidHub Pvt. Ltd., where I had the opportunity to enhance my technical skills and work on practical applications. During this journey, I worked on Python programming, Machine Learning models, Flask web development, Web Scraping, API integration, and Data Analysis. Every project strengthened my problem-solving skills and boosted my confidence in applying theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.
